Organizing Your Trip with TRAVIGO: The Essential Checklist to Do Before Departure.

"That's it, you’ve booked your trip with Evaneos and you’re starting to think about everything that needs to be organized before the big day. Insurance, identification documents, formalities, finances, suitcase… We’ve prepared a checklist so you don’t forget anything, whether it’s 3 months or 2 days before departure."

From the moment you pay the deposit, it’s time to handle the basics.

> Insurance is essential for traveling with peace of mind.

You've just received the email confirming your trip payment, a sign that your dream is becoming a reality. Even with three months until departure, you can already envision yourself there! However, there are some steps to ensure that this trip goes smoothly. First, consider the issue of insurance. Whether you're traveling with family or as a couple, the effort and costs invested in this project deserve protection in case of unforeseen events. To avoid hassles, it’s best to turn to travel experts who offer insurance plans tailored to the specific needs of travelers. This is exactly what Evaneos provides in partnership with AXA and Chapka. For more details on Evaneos’ insurance offers, click here.

> Another crucial step at this stage is to check the validity of your identification documents

allowing enough time to renew them if necessary. While an expired ID card or passport may be valid in France or within the European Union, it won't be accepted outside of these areas. Additionally, a driver's license is not considered a valid form of identification in most countries outside the EU. Typically, you’ll need to present a valid passport, and for some countries, it must be valid for at least six months beyond your return flight date. If you're traveling with young children, make sure to carefully check the validity of their passports, as their expiration dates may be shorter than those of adults.
