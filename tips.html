2 days to visit Cairo:
what to see, what to do?

A bustling megacity of 20 million inhabitants, Cairo reveals multi-millennial treasures. A true journey through time awaits visitors, from the famous Pyramids of Giza to the Coptic churches and Islamic buildings in the historic heart. We guide you through the alleys of the city of a thousand minarets. Each corner tells a story, and every street is filled with vibrant culture and history. Immerse yourself in the local life, where ancient traditions meet the rhythm of modernity.
